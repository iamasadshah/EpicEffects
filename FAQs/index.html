Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform.

What is your platform about?

Our platform helps creators and developers build beautiful digital experiences with modern UI components, templates, and interactive effects that are easy to integrate into any project.

Is it mobile responsive?

Yes. Every component is designed with responsiveness in mind, ensuring a smooth experience across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

Can I use these components commercially?

Absolutely. You can integrate them into personal projects, client work, or commercial products unless a specific license states otherwise.

Do I need any frameworks?

No. Everything is built using pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, making the components lightweight, fast, and framework-independent.
